# Robinson Davis – AI in Finance & Business
Hello, my name is Robinson Davis. I am the Strategic Partnerships Manager at Baltimore Homecoming, a Johns Hopkins MBA student, and I also help run the operations of a gym. My background spans relationship-building, organizational strategy, and day-to-day business management.
I am particularly interested in how generative AI can transform finance and business operations — from streamlining financial analysis to improving how organizations plan, communicate, and grow. As someone managing multiple roles, I see firsthand how time-consuming routine business tasks can be, and I believe AI has real potential to change that.
Three ways generative AI could be useful in finance and business:
- **Financial reporting & analysis:** AI can quickly summarize financial statements, flag trends, and generate narrative reports, saving analysts hours of manual work.
- **Partnership and outreach communications:** AI can draft personalized partnership proposals, follow-up emails, and pitch decks tailored to specific audiences.
- **Operations planning:** AI can help small business operators like gym managers build schedules, forecast demand, and identify inefficiencies in workflows.
